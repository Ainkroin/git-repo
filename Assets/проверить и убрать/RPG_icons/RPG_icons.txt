RPG ICONS
---------

A collection of icons and buttons for use in a standard Fantasy RPG style game.

contents:

8	Character class (128*128 pixels)

12	Male character portraits (128*128 pixels) Generic Human/Elf/Halfling/Dwarf

12	Female character portraits (128*128 pixels) Generic Human/Elf/Halfling/Dwarf

200 	Item icons (96*96 pixels) Weapons, armour, keys, jewellery, torches, coins etc

35 	Skill icons (114*114 pixels) non-magic skills, weapon proficiency, special attacks, luck, charm, mapping etc.
	(also contains pressed and disabled states versions) 

21	Spell icons (114*114 pixels) magic attacks, healing, light cast, etc
	(also contains pressed and disabled states versions) 

86 	Assorted GUI buttons (114*114 pixels) navigation buttons, equipment slots, inspect, throw, drop, pick up etc.


All in PNG format.
